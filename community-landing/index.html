The Curriculum

8 modules. 12 weeks. 1 complete launch plan.

The full curriculum is covered in live coaching calls. Each module builds on the last, moving you from concept validation through pre-launch readiness. All materials are provided and yours to keep forever.

Module 1: Concept Validation

Define your shop concept, validate market demand, identify your ideal customer, and confirm this is the right business for you before you invest.

Module 2: Financial Foundations

Build a complete financial model. Understand unit economics, pricing strategy, break-even analysis, and realistic revenue projections for your market.

Module 3: Location Strategy

Choose the right location using the Location Scorecard. Evaluate foot traffic, demographics, lease terms, and operational fit. Location is destiny in coffee retail.

Module 4: Brand & Positioning

Create a cohesive brand identity. Define your value proposition, messaging, visual identity, and customer experience. Complete your Brand Brief workshop.

Module 5: Menu & Product

Develop your menu using the Menu Development Framework. Source specialty coffee, design your beverage lineup, food strategy, and operational workflows.

Module 6: Legal & Licensing

Navigate permits, licenses, insurance, and legal structures. Complete your Legal Launch Checklist. Know exactly what you need before opening.

Module 7: Operations & Hiring

Build operational systems and hiring playbooks. Staff training, daily procedures, cost control, and the day-to-day reality of running a coffee shop.

Module 8: Pre-Launch & Grand Opening

Execute your Pre-Launch Marketing Playbook. Build buzz, secure your location, finalize all details, and prepare for a strong opening week.

FAQ

Common questions answered.

What exactly do I get?

The complete Coffee Shop Launch System: 8 curriculum modules delivered over 12 weeks via live group coaching calls (12 calls, 90 minutes each), done-with-you templates ($2,400+ in resources), private Discord community for your cohort, and lifetime alumni access to everything.

How does the cohort work?

You join a group of 15–20 people at the same stage. Every week for 12 weeks, we have a live 90-minute coaching call. Each call covers one module with training, live Q&A, and homework for the week. You work through the material at your own pace, but you're not alone — you have a cohort of people building alongside you and a private Discord for daily support.

What if I can't make a live call?

All calls are recorded. If you miss a live session, you can watch the recording and catch up. That said, the live calls are where the magic happens — the accountability, the real questions, the community. We designed this assuming you'll make most calls. If you can't commit to that, this might not be the right fit.

When does the next cohort start?

Cohorts launch quarterly. You can apply anytime, and we'll confirm the start date based on enrollment. We don't start a cohort until we have at least 8 committed members (to ensure depth of discussion) and cap at 20.

Is there really a money-back guarantee?

Yes. Complete all 12 modules. Do every homework assignment. Show up to every coaching call. If you don't walk away with a complete, investor-ready launch plan — I'll coach you 1-on-1 at no extra cost until you do. No time limit. No fine print. You do the work. I guarantee the result.

Do I need coffee experience?

No. Many members start with zero coffee knowledge. The curriculum includes coffee education. What matters is that you're serious about learning and building a great shop. Passion for gathering places matters more than espresso expertise.

What payment options are available?

Three options: pay in full ($1,997), 3 payments of $797, or 6 payments of $497. All options are available for founding cohort members. Choose what works for your cash flow.

What if I'm just exploring? Is this too advanced?

Exploration is a valid stage, and many members started there. The curriculum begins at the foundations. We have people from "just feeling called to do this" through "six months from opening." If you're serious about exploring coffee shop ownership, this is the right program.

Does the Reality Check apply toward the Accelerator?
