About Roast Launch

Our story, our mission, and why we're building this community.

John's Story

I spent years in a 9-to-5 job that paid well but left me spiritually empty. I loved specialty coffee—not as a casual hobby, but as something that awakened me to quality, craft, and purpose. I started drinking third-wave coffee, learning about single-origin beans, extraction science, and the incredible stories behind ethical sourcing. Coffee became more than caffeine; it became a way to think about creating something meaningful.

But there was a gap. I wanted to open a specialty coffee shop, but I didn't know where to start. I couldn't find a resource that bridged the gap between coffee passion and business reality. So I did what I always do—I researched obsessively. I studied the specialty coffee industry, interviewed dozens of coffee shop owners, dove deep into financial modeling, and explored how to ground this whole venture in faith, not just hustle culture.

Here's where I'm radically transparent: I don't own a coffee shop yet. But that gives me something valuable. I'm bringing rigorous research, genuine expertise in coffee and business fundamentals, and deep conviction about the importance of faith in entrepreneurship. I'm not selling you a romanticized version of coffee shop ownership. I'm building Roast Launch with people who are serious about learning the coffee, understanding the business, and building something that honors their calling.

What Makes Roast Launch Different

Research-Driven

We don't sell guesswork. Every module is built on real industry data, conversations with successful shop owners, and tested business frameworks. You'll learn what actually works, not what sounds good in theory.

Faith-Grounded

This isn't another hustle-culture course. We believe your coffee shop can be both profitable and purpose-driven. We explore biblical stewardship, calling, and how to build a business that honors your faith and serves your community.

Community-First

You don't launch a coffee shop alone. You need accountability, expertise, and friends who understand the journey. Our community of 100+ members provides exactly that—connection with people who are serious about this calling.

Our Values

Quality First

We're obsessed with the best specialty coffee because quality matters. In our teaching, our community, and everything we build—quality comes before shortcuts.

Radical Transparency

No fake success stories. No overselling. Just honest conversations about what it takes, what the challenges are, and how faith meets you in the middle of risk.

Purpose Over Profit

We believe your business can be profitable and meaningful. Your coffee shop isn't just a revenue stream—it's a platform to serve, build community, and honor your calling.

Stewardship

From ethical sourcing to financial responsibility to serving your team well—we teach business as an act of stewardship, not extraction.
