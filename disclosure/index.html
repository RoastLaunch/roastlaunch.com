Earnings Disclaimer

Roast Launch does not guarantee any specific financial results, business outcomes, or earnings from the use of our products, services, or educational content.

Roast Launch provides educational products and services designed to help aspiring coffee shop owners plan and prepare for opening a specialty coffee shop. Our products — including the Reality Check ($97) and the Coffee Shop Launch Accelerator ($997) — are educational programs that provide knowledge, frameworks, templates, and community support for coffee shop planning.

Our programs teach you how to build a launch plan. They do not guarantee that you will open a coffee shop, secure funding, generate revenue, or achieve any particular financial outcome. Opening and operating a coffee shop is a complex business endeavor with significant financial risk, and outcomes depend on many factors entirely outside of our control.

Any references to financial figures on our website, in our marketing materials, or in our educational content — such as typical coffee shop startup costs, revenue ranges, or industry statistics — are provided for educational context only and do not represent promises or projections of what you will earn or spend.

Factors That Affect Outcomes

Your results from using any Roast Launch product or service will depend on a wide range of factors, including but not limited to: your personal effort, dedication, and follow-through; your local market conditions and competition; your available capital and access to financing; your prior business experience and skills; the location, size, and concept of your coffee shop; general economic conditions; and countless other variables that we cannot predict or control.

We believe in the value of the education and planning tools we provide, but we want to be clear: no educational program can guarantee business success. The responsibility for your business decisions and their outcomes rests entirely with you.

Roast Launch is a new business. As of the date of this disclosure, we have not yet had paying clients complete our programs. We therefore have no client results data to report.

We will never fabricate results, inflate numbers, or use misleading statistics. When we do have client outcomes to share, they will be presented honestly and in full context.

Not a Business Opportunity

Roast Launch is an educational company. We sell educational courses and planning tools. We are not selling a business opportunity, franchise, license, distributorship, or investment.

When you purchase a Roast Launch product, you are purchasing access to educational content, planning templates, and community resources. You are not purchasing a turnkey business, a guaranteed revenue stream, or a promise of employment or income.

Opening a coffee shop requires significant additional investment beyond the cost of our programs — typically ranging from $80,000 to $300,000 or more depending on your location, concept, and build-out scope. Our programs help you plan for this investment; they do not fund it, guarantee it, or replace the need for independent due diligence and professional advice.

Nothing in our marketing materials, website content, emails, social media posts, or educational content should be interpreted as a promise that purchasing our products will result in a profitable business. We provide education and tools. What you do with them is up to you.
