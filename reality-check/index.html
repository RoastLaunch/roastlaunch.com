Questions About the Reality Check

How long does it take?

The course is designed for 5 days at 30–45 minutes per day. You can move faster or slower — it’s entirely self-paced. Most people finish within a week.

What if I don’t have a specific location yet?

That’s fine. Day 2 teaches you how to evaluate locations, so you can use it to assess neighborhoods you’re considering. The framework works whether you have a specific address or a general area in mind.

How does the $97 Accelerator credit work?

If you decide to join the Accelerator after completing the Reality Check, your $97 is applied as a credit toward the $997 founding enrollment (regular price $1,497). You’d pay the remaining $900 (or use the 3-payment plan at an adjusted rate). The credit never expires.

What if my Reality Check says “no-go”?

Then it just saved you $150,000+. A no-go result doesn’t mean your dream is over — it means your current concept, in your current location, with your current resources, needs adjustment. Many people pivot their concept based on what they learn and come back stronger.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — a Completion Guarantee. Complete all five days (do the work and submit it in the course dashboard) within 30 days of purchase, and if the Reality Check didn’t give you the clarity promised, you can request a full refund from your dashboard. This is a completion guarantee, not a try-before-you-buy refund — the course only works if you do the work. And if you do: your $97 is also credited toward the Accelerator, so either way you’re covered.

Do I need the Reality Check before the Accelerator?

No. If you already know your concept is viable and you’re ready to build a full launch plan, you can go straight to the Accelerator. The Reality Check is designed for people who want to validate their idea first.
