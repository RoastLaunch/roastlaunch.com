FAQ

Questions About the Workshop

Is this a sales pitch?

No. You'll get 90 minutes of genuine, valuable education. At the very end, I'll tell you about the next steps available (Reality Check, Accelerator, Newsletter) if you want to go deeper. But the workshop itself is pure education.

Do I need coffee experience?

Not at all. This workshop is designed for complete beginners. If you can describe what specialty coffee is and why it matters, you're ahead of where you need to be.

What if I can't make the live session?

Register anyway. You'll get a recording link afterward so you can watch on your own time. The live Q&A is great, but the content itself is valuable whether you attend live or watch later.

How do I register?

Click the "Register for the Workshop" button above. It takes 60 seconds. You'll get a Zoom link via email for the next session.

What comes after the workshop?

It depends on where you are. Some people join the Newsletter to keep learning. Some take the free Readiness Quiz. Some are ready for the 5-Day Reality Check ($97 — and that $97 applies as credit if you later join the Accelerator) or the self-paced Accelerator ($997). There's no pressure — just the next step that makes sense for you.
