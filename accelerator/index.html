FAQ

Common Questions

I’m not sure I’ll actually open a shop. Is it worth $997 if I’m still deciding?

That’s exactly who this is for. Module 1 is literally called “The Vision & Calling” — it helps you figure out whether this is a hobby, a dream, or a genuine calling worth pursuing. Most people who join are still deciding. The Accelerator gives you the real numbers and the honest framework to make that decision with confidence instead of guesswork. And if you do the work and it doesn’t deliver a fundable plan, you get your money back.

Do I need coffee experience to join?

Not at all. The Accelerator is designed for aspiring coffee shop owners starting from scratch. Module 1 begins with your vision and calling — no barista skills required. You’ll learn everything from specialty sourcing to financial modeling along the way.

How long does it take to complete?

The program is designed for 8–10 weeks at your own pace. Some people complete it faster, others take longer. There’s no deadline — you have lifetime access to all materials.

What’s the difference between founding and standard price?

Identical program. The $997 founding price is available until May 1, 2026 and will increase to $1,497 after that date. Lock in the founding price now and keep it forever — including all future updates and content additions.

How does the 3-payment plan work?

Instead of one payment of $997, you can split it into 3 monthly payments of $367. You get full access from day one. Payments are processed automatically each month.

What does the money-back guarantee mean?

Complete all 8 modules, submit all worksheets, participate in community Q&A, and fill out all 7 deliverable templates. If you don’t walk away with a complete, fundable launch plan, you get a full refund. The guarantee exists because the system works when you show up.

How does community Q&A work?

You post questions anytime in the community at app.roastlaunch.com. John responds with detailed text, audio, or video answers — and everyone learns from the thread. It’s more thorough than a live call because you get thoughtful, detailed responses.

Should I do the Reality Check first?

If you’re not sure whether your concept is viable, start with the $97 Reality Check. It’s a 5-day validation course, and the $97 is credited toward the Accelerator. If you already know you’re ready to build a full plan, go straight to the Accelerator.

Is this faith-based?

Faith is part of John’s story and the Roast Launch ethos — we believe a coffee shop can be a calling, not just a business. But the program is built on hard data, real frameworks, and proven business strategy. You don’t need to share any particular belief to get enormous value from the Accelerator.

How much does a coffee shop actually cost to open?

Typical specialty coffee shop buildouts range from $80,000 to $300,000+ depending on location, size, and scope. Module 3 teaches you to model your specific numbers so you know exactly what you need before you sign a lease. Try our free Startup Calculator for a quick estimate.
