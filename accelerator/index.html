FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need coffee experience to join?

No. The Accelerator is designed for people who are passionate about opening a coffee shop but haven't done it yet. We start from the very beginning — your vision and calling — and build from there. Coffee craft is part of the curriculum, not a prerequisite.

What if I'm not sure I'm ready?

That's exactly what Module 1 is for. "The Vision & Calling" helps you honestly evaluate whether this is a hobby, a dream, or a genuine calling — and count the real cost before you commit. Plus, you have 30 days to try the program risk-free.

How long does it take to complete?

It's entirely self-paced. Most people move through one module every 1-2 weeks, which means you could have your complete launch plan in 2-4 months. But there's no rush — you have lifetime access, so you can go as fast or slow as your schedule allows.

What does the 100% guarantee actually mean?

Two things. First: within the first 30 days, you can get a full refund for any reason at all, no questions asked. Second: if you complete all 8 modules and do the exercises but don't end up with a launch plan you're confident in, we'll refund your full purchase price. You literally can't lose.

How much does a coffee shop actually cost to open?

Typical startup costs range from $80,000 to $300,000+ depending on size, location, and buildout scope. Module 3 (Business Model & Finance) walks you through building exact projections for your specific concept — so you know your real number before you commit a dollar.

Is this faith-based?

Roast Launch is built on faith-grounded principles — the belief that a coffee shop can serve a community with purpose and generosity. But you don't need to share a specific faith to benefit. If you want to build something meaningful, you belong here.

What happens after May 1?

The founding price of $997 ends on May 1, 2026. After that, the Accelerator moves to its regular price of $1,497. If you're already enrolled, your price is locked in forever — including access to all future updates and additions.
