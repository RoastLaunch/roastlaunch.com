FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

What's the best way to get started?

Start with the Free Workshop or the Free Readiness Quiz. Both are zero-commitment ways to explore whether opening a coffee shop is right for you. From there, you can decide if you want to go deeper with the 5-Day Reality Check or the full Accelerator program.

Do I need to do the Reality Check before the Accelerator?

No, it's not required. We recommend it because it's a great way to validate your concept before making a larger investment. But if you're already confident and ready to build, you can jump straight into the Accelerator.

How does the community work?

You get access to our in-app member community at app.roastlaunch.com where you can connect with other founders, ask questions, and share your progress. We also record monthly Q&A videos — submit your questions and get detailed video answers you can watch anytime.

Is there a guarantee?

Yes — both products are guaranteed. For the Accelerator: 100% Money-Back Guarantee. Complete all 8 modules, do all the exercises, and submit all assignments. If you don't walk away with a complete, fundable launch plan, you get a full refund. For the 5-Day Reality Check: 30-Day Completion Guarantee. Complete all 5 days (with your worksheet submissions) within 30 days of purchase, and if the Reality Check didn't give you the clarity promised, you can request a full refund from your dashboard. Either way, if you decide to continue with the Accelerator, your $97 Reality Check purchase credits toward enrollment.

What if I decide opening a coffee shop isn't for me?

That's valuable too. The Reality Check and Accelerator are designed to help you discover whether this is the right path for you — and if it's not, knowing that sooner is worth far more than the investment. That's why we have guarantees.

What makes this different from other coffee shop courses?

We combine specialty coffee education, real financial modeling, community accountability, and faith as a foundation. This isn't a surface-level course — it's an 8-module deep dive with expert-led video lessons, templates built specifically for launching a coffee shop, and a community of people committed to the same vision.
