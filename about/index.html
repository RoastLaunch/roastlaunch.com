About Roast Launch

Our story, our mission, and why community is at the center of everything we do.

John's Story

I spent years in a 9-to-5 job that paid well but left me spiritually empty. When I discovered specialty coffee, something shifted — not just because of the craft, but because of the community. Every great coffee shop I visited was a gathering place first. People came for the coffee and stayed for the connection. I realized that's what I wanted to build — not just a business, but a place where people belong.

But I hit a wall. I couldn't find a single resource that connected coffee craft with community building and sound business fundamentals. Everything was either too technical or too abstract. So I did what I always do — I researched obsessively, talked to dozens of shop owners, and started building the roadmap I wish had existed.

Here's where I'm radically transparent: I don't own a coffee shop yet. But I've poured that research into building something just as important — a community of people who share this calling. Roast Launch isn't just me teaching you. It's all of us building together, sharing what we learn, holding each other accountable, and proving that you don't have to do this alone.

What Makes Roast Launch Different

Community-First

You don't build a coffee shop alone, and you don't launch a community-centered business in isolation. Roast Launch is built around the people — weekly calls, shared learning, accountability partnerships, and genuine friendships with others who understand exactly what you're building toward.

Faith-Grounded

A great gathering place serves its community with purpose. We believe your coffee shop can be both profitable and meaningful — a space where people feel welcome, where relationships deepen, and where your faith shows up in how you treat every person who walks through the door.

Research-Driven

Every module is built on real industry data, conversations with successful shop owners, and tested business frameworks. We don't sell guesswork. You'll learn what actually works — so the gathering place you create is built to last.

Our Values

Community First

The best coffee shops aren't defined by their espresso — they're defined by how people feel when they walk in. We build community into everything: our curriculum, our calls, our forum, and the shops we're helping create.

Radical Transparency

No fake success stories. No overselling. Just honest conversations about what it takes, what the challenges are, and how faith meets you in the middle of risk.

Purpose Over Profit

Your coffee shop isn't just a revenue stream — it's a gathering place. A space where neighbors become friends, where people find belonging, and where your community grows stronger one cup at a time.

Stewardship

From ethical sourcing to serving your team well to caring for your neighborhood — we teach business as an act of stewardship toward the community you're called to serve.
