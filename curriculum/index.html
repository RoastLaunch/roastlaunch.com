The Coffee Shop Launch System

8 modules. Self-paced. A complete launch plan — built with expert-led video lessons and a community of founders building alongside you.

01

The Vision & Calling

Before you build a business, you need to understand why — and who you're building it for. Discover what kind of gathering place your community needs. Explore whether this is a hobby, a dream, or a genuine calling. Count the cost honestly.

02

Market Research & Location

Find the neighborhood that needs you. Learn market evaluation, competitive analysis, foot traffic patterns, and how to identify communities that are underserved by quality gathering spaces. Your location determines the community you'll serve.

03

Business Model & Finance

Build the financial model that keeps your gathering place open for the long haul. Startup costs, revenue projections, break-even analysis, funding options, and an honest plan for transitioning from your 9-to-5 without putting your family at risk.

04

Brand & Identity

Create a brand that reflects your community and invites people to belong. Your name, your story, your visual identity, and your menu philosophy should all communicate: this is a place for you.

05

Operations & Training

Build systems that create consistency and warmth at scale. Coffee sourcing, equipment, staffing, SOPs, and the team culture that makes every visit feel like coming home — not just another transaction.

06

Legal & Administrative

Handle the structure so you can focus on people. LLC formation, permits, licenses, insurance, banking, and accounting — the unglamorous work that protects the community you're building.

07

Pre-Launch Marketing & Community

Build your community before you open the doors. Pre-launch strategy, social media, email list building, local partnerships, and a grand opening that feels like a neighborhood reunion.

08

Launch & First 90 Days

Open your doors with confidence and a community already cheering you on. Soft opening strategy, managing first operations, sustainability systems, and a 90-day review to make sure you're building something that lasts.

FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

How long does it take?

The Accelerator is an 8-module self-paced program. Most members complete it in 4–8 weeks, but you can move through it at whatever pace works for you.

Do I need prior coffee experience?

No. Module 1 (The Vision & Calling) starts from the beginning and builds genuine expertise, even if you're new to specialty coffee. Many of our members started as coffee enthusiasts and learned the deeper education through the program. That said, deep curiosity about coffee is essential.

What's included in the Accelerator?

8 modules of expert-led video lessons, 7 done-with-you professional templates, member community access at app.roastlaunch.com, direct Q&A with John (text, audio, or video), and the 100% Launch Plan Guarantee. That's $11,500+ in total value for a $997 investment — less than 1% of what the average failed coffee shop loses.

Can I get a refund if the Accelerator isn't right for me?

Yes. We offer the 100% Money-Back Guarantee: complete all 8 modules, do the exercises, submit all assignments, and if you don't walk away with a complete, fundable launch plan — full refund. No fine print, no hoops. We believe in this program because it works.

What if I'm not ready to open a shop yet?

Perfect. The curriculum is designed for people at different stages. Some members are exploring whether a coffee shop is their calling. Others are two years out. The modules help you validate your idea, build expertise, and connect with a community that makes the journey possible — wherever you are right now.
