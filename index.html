Common Questions

Do I need coffee experience to join the Accelerator?

Not at all. The Accelerator is designed for aspiring coffee shop owners starting from scratch. Module 1 begins with your vision and calling — no barista skills required. You'll learn everything from specialty sourcing to financial modeling along the way.

What if I'm not sure I'm ready?

That's exactly what the free workshop and $97 Reality Check are for. Start with the workshop to learn what kills coffee shops before they open. If you want to go deeper, the Reality Check gives you 5 days of focused validation — and that $97 applies as credit toward the Accelerator.

What does the 100% guarantee mean?

Complete all 8 modules and do the exercises. If you don't walk away with a complete, fundable launch plan, you get a full refund. The guarantee exists because the system works when you show up.

How much does a coffee shop actually cost to open?

Typical specialty coffee shop buildouts range from $80,000 to $300,000+ depending on location, size, and build-out scope. The Accelerator teaches you to model your specific numbers so you know exactly what you need before you sign a lease. Try our free Calculator for a quick estimate.

Is this faith-based?

Faith is part of John's story and the Roast Launch ethos — we believe a coffee shop can be a calling, not just a business. But the program is built on hard data, real frameworks, and proven business strategy. You don't need to share any particular belief to get enormous value from the Accelerator.
