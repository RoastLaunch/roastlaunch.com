Information collected automatically:

GA4 may collect your IP address (which Google anonymizes), approximate geographic location, and browsing interactions. This data helps us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.

Meta Pixel collects data about your visits to our site and actions you take (such as viewing a page or completing a purchase) and sends this information to Meta Platforms, Inc. This data may be used to show you relevant ads on Facebook and Instagram, and to measure ad performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We do not rent, trade, or share your email address with anyone outside of Roast Launch, except as described in the Third-Party Services section below.
