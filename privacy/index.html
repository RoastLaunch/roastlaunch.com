Privacy Policy

Roast Launch, LLC ("Roast Launch," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit roastlaunch.com and use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter or join the community
  • Payment information — when you purchase a membership (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store your card details)
  • Name — if you provide it when joining the community or contacting us
  • Quiz responses — when you take the Coffee Shop Readiness Assessment (not tied to your identity unless you provide your email)

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages viewed. This data is collected through standard web analytics and is used in aggregate to understand how our site is used.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our weekly newsletter and community updates
  • Process membership payments and manage your account
  • Provide access to curriculum, community forums, and member resources
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve our website, content, and services

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We do not rent, trade, or share your email address with anyone outside of Roast Launch.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Beehiiv — for email newsletter delivery and subscription management
  • Stripe — for secure payment processing
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their policies.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. All payment processing is handled by Stripe using industry-standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter using the link at the bottom of any email
  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Cancel your membership

To exercise any of these rights, email us at john@roastlaunch.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
